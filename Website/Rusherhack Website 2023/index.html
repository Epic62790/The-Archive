RusherHack is a Player Assistance Utility Mod designed for anarchy servers like 2b2t.org and oldfrog.org
RusherHack is not a "cheat client", or a "hacked client", it is a utility/player assistance mod (similar to OptiFine, Impact, 5zig, Labymod, and countless others).
Please note that mods like this are not allowed on some servers, and we will not permit any usage outside of anarchy servers.
